Allen Maxwell

Hello Everyone! My name is Allen Maxwell. I currently live in San Jose, California. During the day, you can find me at a local auto parts warehouse called Allied Auto Stores. I work in the wholesale parts department where we sell parts to all the General Motors dealerships throughout the Bay Area. Aside from selling auto parts, you can find me restoring classic cars. I am currently working on a 1971 Chevrolet Camaro and a 1960 Chevrolet Corvette. Besides cars, I also play the drums in a Rock n Roll band called The Last Outlaws.

Furthermore, I also enjoy many outdoor activities. You cannot beat these amazing outdoors that fullfill our beautiful valley. That being said, in the winter time you can find me shredding down the mountains in Tahoe and in the summer I'll be catching some sun on the beaches in Santa Cruz. Oh, you can't forget about the amazing redwoods up and down the coast; great for camping and hiking. Then at then end of the day, you can wash it all down with a fine craft beer from any one of the many local breweries. That's me in a nutshell, so maybe I'll see you on the next adventure!!
