Allen Maxwell

Hello, my name is Allen Maxwell and I am focused on becoming a full stack web developer. Currently I’ve been working in the automotive field for over ten years. I’ve done everything from managing a parts store, provide exceptional customer service, and diagnose cars. Now, I’m excited for the next step in my life; web developing.

All my life I’ve enjoyed building things, whether it be Legos, classic cars, music, or web developing, I have always had a creative passion. I have devoted hundreds of hours learning multiple modern programing languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Firebase and Node. Through these studies I have created this website and below are links to some of my projects I am very proud of. Early in my programming career I developed a few simple games such as hangman and a crystal collector game. I have also worked in a group organization via GitHub where we each created our own branch off master, made multiple commits, and created pull requests. In this group we collaborated to create an awesome app where you can find local events via geolocation, the user can save these events to their personal page, and even find tickets through a ticket link.

Apart from web developing, I enjoy outdoor activities and spending time with my lovely wife, Silvia. Silvia and I push each other to obtain a greater education and have high goals. The constant support is invaluable to success in life and I am very grateful. I also am known to be a decent chef. I’ve been told I can make an exceptional set of smoked BBQ pork spareribs.

Please reach out to me if your interested in me developing a website for you or your company, or if you want to swing by for some top-notch smoke ribs. Either way, reach out to me in the contact me page of this site. See you soon!
